Dalgona Coffee

This dalgona coffee AKA whipped coffe is a south Korean coffee treat! It used to be the internet's favorite and most popular treat to make.

Ingredients

  • Instant Coffee Powder
  • Sugar
  • Hot water
  • Milk

Dalgona Making Process

>
Step 1: Combine the instant coffee, sugar, and hot water
Add tablespoons of instant coffee, 2 tablespoons of sugar, and 2 tablespoons of hot water to a medium mixing bowl
Step 2: Whisk, Whisk, Whisk!
Keep whisking till the coffe mixture is light and fluffy. It is much easier if you whip it with a hand mixer, but if you don't have one you can always use a whisk and spoon
>
Step 3: Put milk in a mason jar or any glass, and then put the whipped coffee mixture in the glass
You can serve with a straw and put additional toppings
